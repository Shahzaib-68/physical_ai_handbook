## üìö Book Structure

This book consists of 10 chapters covering fundamental concepts in Physical AI:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI**: First steps with interactive physics simulations
2. **Kinematics Fundamentals**: Understanding motion in Physical AI
3. **Dynamics - Forces & Motion**: How forces affect movement
4. **Sensors**: How robots perceive their environment
5. **Control Systems**: Making robots do what you want
6. **Motion Planning**: Navigating through space
7. **Learning in Physical Systems**: AI for robotics
8. **Robot Vision**: Understanding the visual world
9. **Manipulation**: Grasping and moving objects
10. **Humanoid Robots**: Bringing it all together
