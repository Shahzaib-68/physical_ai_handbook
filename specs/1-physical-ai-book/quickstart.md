2. Click "Run" to see your changes
3. When you have something you like, click "Save Project" to store it
4. You can always come back to your saved projects later

### Troubleshooting

**Demo not loading?**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache if problems persist

**Code not working as expected?**
- Make sure your modifications follow the syntax of the original code
- Check browser console (F12) for error messages
- Look at the community comments for common solutions
